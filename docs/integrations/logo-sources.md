## üìã Implementation Steps:

### Method 1: Direct Download
1. Visit each organization's website
2. Right-click their logo in the header
3. Select "Copy image address" or "Save image as"
4. Upload to your server/hosting

### Method 2: Hotlinking (Use Carefully)
1. Copy the direct image URL from their website
2. Use in your widget (check their terms of service first)
3. Some sites block hotlinking, so test thoroughly

### Method 3: Create Your Own
1. Use their brand colors and fonts
2. Create simple text-based logos
3. Ensures no copyright issues
4. Maintains consistent styling
